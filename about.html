about maeve.

 

 

I'm a Melbourne based journo with experience in a wide range of mediums, but a flair for feature and colour writing. I'm also a fashion, art and culture devotee, and pride myself on being an engaging and entertaining storyteller.

I've worked as an intern at Fashion Journal and Issimo Magazine, where I've written and published a number of short and longform pieces, and at the Lifted Brow, where I was involved in the nitty gritty work behind the scenes of a magazine.

I'm currently completing a Bachelor of Communication (Journalism) at RMIT University, graduating at the end of 2018.
